Restcycle Animated Gifs

Adobe Photoshop, After Effects, Ezgifs
Spring 2018

Overview

When I worked in Mass Minority agency, one day my Art director gave me a task to style a wordpress blog. The purpose of the blog is to write interesting articles about how sleep quality can affect your life. I get contents from the content creators and did mark up to the blog. But it still looks boring. So we brainstormed and decided to anime the cover images.

I got those images from the artist and perpared them in different layers in Photoshop as the first step of the further alteration.

To fit the topic of sleep, I collabrated with our data scientist and got some ideas of the vibe for the animation, it should be calm and smoothly just like the mood for sleep. With that in mind I made those animations.

The Challenge

As this project got more posts, time was an essense so I have to work around the clock for it. The challenge include the fast approch deadlines but also how can I compress those Gifs to be the smallest but also work smoothly. I spent tons of time to edit those gifs and cut off frames.

The Outcome

I would say I am glad to see how they turn out. I admit if I have more time I will probably work on it more to have complex actions, but I am not dispointted of the outcome.

The website still runs today, as an interesting piece of the Simmons restcycle campaign.
